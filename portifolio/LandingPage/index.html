MCU module that targets a wide variety of applications!

ESP32 is a powerful, MCU module that targets a wide variety of applications,ranging from low-power sensor networks to the most demanding tasks, such as voice encoding, music streamingand MP3 decoding.

ESP32 have a powerful hardware and can be conected in internet, see all Hardware Specifications:

  • Wifi - Protocols: 802.11 b/g/n (802.11n up to 150 Mbps)
  • Bluetooth* - Protocols: Bluetooth v4.2 BR/EDR and BLE specification
  • Module interfaces: 2 Cores*, SD card, UART, SPI, SDIO, I2C, LED PWM, Motor PWM*, I2S,IR *, pulse counter, GPIO, capacitive touch sensor *, ADC, DAC*

OBS:
*: Only in esp32

The ESP Family can be programmed for a lot of languagens, See!
