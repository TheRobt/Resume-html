Engineer Nikolas Tesla

Inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system!

  • 1856 - Born in the Austrian Empire (present day Croatia)
  • 1887 - Developed an induction motor that used on alternating current (AC), a power system format that was rapidly expanding in Europe and the United States because of its advantages in long-distance, high-voltage transmission. The motor used polyphase current, which generated a rotating magnetic field to turn the motor (a principle that Tesla claimed to have conceived in 1882)
  • 1891 - Patented a Tesla coil is a radio frequency oscillator that drives an air-core double-tuned resonant transformer to produce high voltages at low current;
  • 1894 - Began investiganting radiant energy of "invisible" kinds, later indentified as "X-Rays"
  • 1896 - Demonstrated a boat that used a coherer-based radio control—which he dubbed "telautomaton"—to the public during an electrical exhibition at Madison Square Garden.[133] The crowd that witnessed the demonstration made outrageous claims about the workings of the boat, such as magic, telepathy, and being piloted by a trained monkey hidden inside.
